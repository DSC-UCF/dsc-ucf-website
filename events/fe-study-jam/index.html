Event Information Learn more about GDSC UCF and get cramming with us August 15th at 1PM for a Foundation Exam Study Jam and Info Session! We’ll be going over info about the club and then focusing on Foundation Exam prep, running through practice problems, handing out a cheat sheet, and covering tips, tricks, and common mistakes.

In this comprehensive session, we’ll first go over info about GDSC UCF and talk about:

  • What the Google Developer Student Club at the University of Central Florida is
  • Why/how you can join
  • Opportunities to apply for leadership roles within the club.

Next we'll dive into a Foundation Exam study session, covering tips, tricks, and common mistakes. We’ll coach you on how to approach the variety of questions, run through a problem set, and hand out a cheat sheet! This meeting will be recorded and all resources made available will be provided later on, in case you can’t make it. Study on!
