Getting Started with Google Cloud

Workshop November 21st, 2020 (2:00pm - 4:00pm Eastern)

Join the UCF and FAU Google Developer Student Clubs and follow along as we explore some of the fundamental concepts and APIs available in the Google Cloud Platform. We'll introduce the Qwiklabs platform and the Google Cloud Platform Console, then dive into creating virtual machines with Compute Engine, setting up Kubernetes clusters with the Google Kubernetes Engine, and then explore networking and HTTP load balancing.

After completing this event, members will have earned a badge to show off on their LinkedIn and social media profiles. Members can optionally complete a challenge lab to earn an additional badge.

Solution Challenge

Projects

The Google Solution Challenge is an annual competition open to the university students under the mentorship umbrella of a GDSC chapter. The solution must feature one or more Google technologies and must address a local problem faced by a business, organization, NGO, etc.
