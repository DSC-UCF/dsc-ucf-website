Cloud Study Jam 1: GCP Essentials

We hope you enjoyed our very first Cloud Study Jam! We focused Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and participants got hands-on practice through Qwiklabs creating virtual machines with Compute Engine, working with clusters in Google Kubernetes Engine, and configuring web servers and load balancers. Members also had the chance to test their skills by completing a challenge lab.

Didn't make it?

If you didn't make it to this first Study Jam, that's alright. We have many more exciting events planned for the future. Make sure to fill out the contact form if you're not in the Discord so you can recieve updates and communicate with other members.

Attended?

If you did attend, we would love to hear your feedback! Check the Discord Study Jam channel for the link to the form. Make sure to let us know what topics you would like us to cover in future events!

What's next?

As a reminder, by completing the first lab of the quest, you should have received 30 days to complete the rest of the quest. Upon completing the rest of the GCP Essentials quest, you will recieve an additional 30 days of unlimited access to Qwiklabs quest, allowing you to explore whatever topics you wish to learn.

Getting help

If you need any help with completing the labs, have any questions about the content, or wish to know more about what was discussed (or need help with additional quests), make sure to ask in the Discord server. Our Tech Leads have gone through the content and can provide a more extensive information if requested.
Take the opportunity to discuss these topics with other members in the club. Get to know them, ask questions, and provide help. This is a great way to find potential team members for the Solution Challenge. Make sure to guide members to the correct solution rather than giving them the answer.
