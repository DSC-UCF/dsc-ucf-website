Cloud Study Jam 1: GCP Essentials

We hope you enjoyed our very first Cloud Study Jam! Using Qwiklabs, our participants got hands-on practice with the Google Cloud Platform (GCP) by creating virtual machines, working with Kubernetes clusters, and configuring load balancers. Members tested their skills by completing a challenge lab.

Didn't make it?

If you didn't make it to this first Study Jam, that's alright. We have many more exciting events planned for the future. Make sure to fill out the contact form and join our Discord so you can recieve updates and communicate with other members.

What's next?

After starting the first lab, you should have received 30 days to complete the rest of the quest. Upon completing the rest of the GCP Essentials quest, you will recieve an additional 30 days of unlimited access to Qwiklabs quests, allowing you to explore whatever topics you wish to learn.

Getting help

If you need any help with completing the labs or have any questions about the content, feel free to ask a Tech Lead in our Discord server. Our Tech Leads have gone through the content and can provide more extensive information as requested.
Take the opportunity to discuss these topics with other members in the club. Get to know them, ask questions, and provide help. This is a great way to find potential team members for the Solution Challenge. Make sure to guide members to the correct solution rather than giving them the answer.

To continue working on the labs and other quests, you may click the button below. You should NOT need to pay for access to any labs.
